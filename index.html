Board Game Designers Ireland

We are a collective of board game designers, developing, publishing and promoting Irish-made tabletop experiences.

Join one of our events

About Us

We help you develop your games

Likeminded designers from a variety of backgrounds help one-another invent, playtest, and develop each others games. Bring any games you've been working on - no matter where you are in their design process - and get some fresh eyes and fresh ideas!

We give publishing advice

Many members of Board Game Designers Ireland have experience with publishing games and know of the unique challenges facing Irish publishers. Whether you want to self-publish a game through crowdfunding or pitch your game to more traditional publishing houses, we can advise you on common pitfalls, best practices, and provide industry contacts.

We promote Irish-made games

A rising tide lifts all ships, so we work hard to raise the profile of Irish-made tabletop games. We hold annual public facing events, combine resources to attend international industry events (like the UK Games Expo and Essen Spiel), and give a positive face to our industry in the media.
